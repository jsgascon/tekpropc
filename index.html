Computer Viruses

Computer Viruses can do great harm to your computer and amount for approximately 70% of the computer issues we get in our shop. We can perform virus removals 2 different ways.
The preferred option is to do a Wipe & Reload with a data backup. With this option we take all the files on your computer and place them in a backup folder. Then we reinstall your Windows operating system. With this option you will need to reinstall any software you have. We're happy to assist you with this if you need us to. The best benefit of this approach is it guarantees that the viruses you had are not in your operating system.
The second approach we can attempt is to perform virus scans with a different computer. It is essential to perform the scan on a different operating system to allow the viruses to be removed. This approach is about 95% effective because not all viruses are known. We still guarantee our work for 30 days.

DC Jack Repair

It seems to be the case that the laptops we all use are not as portable as we would like. They almost always have a cord attached. The DC Jack is very susceptible to breaking due to the laptop being dropped on the cord or pulling on the cord.
At TekPro PC we are experienced in DC Jack Replacement. If you already have a loose connection, please do not wait to bring it in. This situation can often rip out the soldier eyes from the motherboard of you laptop and make your notebook a brick. This service is only $100 and it includes the DC Jack.

Data Recovery

We're even able, in most instances, to recover your data off of failing hard drives, corrupted file systems, formatted hard drives, or deleted files.
